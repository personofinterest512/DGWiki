# vmfunc

another kid the school system didn't know what to do with. too fast, too weird, too many questions nobody wanted to answer. but the machines always answered. open a hex editor and the whole world makes sense... open IDA and it tells you everything they didn't want you to know.

started writing game cheats because nobody said she couldn't. aimbots, wallhacks, memory patches. they called it cheating, she called it her first disassembly class. one thing leads to another and now she writes x86 from the psych ward and hacks systems for reasons the news would call "political."

hacktivist. reverse engineer. the girl reading your firmware while you sleep. owns her own boxes, runs her own ASN, her own ISP, 11 deep and counting. your packets are her packets. all information should be free and if it isn't she'll free it herself.

my crime is that of curiosity and i'll never stop committing it.
